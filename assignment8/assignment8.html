'Timless Fashion'

2023 Edition

Topic

I have a strong passion for fashion and decided to develop a website showcasing various outfit ideas tailored to different seasons - summer, fall, spring, and winter. The website will feature approximately 4-5 outfits for each season,complete with details about the source of each clothing item, enabling viewers to easily access and acquire these stylish garments.

Audience

People that are interested in fashion, more specifically those around 18-25 years of age.

Design & Style

I prefer a limited color scheme since the vibrant outfit colors could create excessive contrast so I'd like to opt for a neutral, light color palette. My idea is to assign distinct color palettes to each season section, such as using a deep olive green for spring text and a muted grayish blue for winter text and probably a white or silverish background for all.
